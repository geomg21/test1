Kathakali is a classical Indian dance-drama that originated in Kerala. It is known for its elaborate costumes, vibrant makeup, intricate hand gestures (mudras), and expressive facial expressions.

Thrissur Pooram is one of the most famous and grandest temple festivals in Kerala, celebrated with much pomp and splendor in the city of Thrissur..
